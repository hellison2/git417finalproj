What Is Red Rock Fest?

Red Rock Fest is an annual music festival that takes place in the invigorating red desert rock formations of southern Utah. Featuring the best rock and metal bands of the modern era, you're guaranteed to have a good time!

This year's event will be hosted at Red Cliff's Adventure Lodge. Prepare for a weekend of incredible bands surrounded by surreal scenery!

Enjoy three jam-packed days of awesome artists, activities, and vendors. Mosh in the pit during the sets and explore local artists and art shows as you walk between stages. Stay at the lodge or camp under the vast stars of the desert sky.

Dates:

Friday, September 20th, 2024 - Sunday, September 22nd, 2024

Address:

Red Cliff's Adventure Lodge

Mile Post, 14 UT-128

Moab, UT 84532
